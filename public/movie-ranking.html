My Top Movies of 2023

My favorite top three movies of all time.

No One Will Save You

This movie will be the top out of the three. The way it portrayed the aliens as creepy and glitchy looking.

The ending gave a different perspective as the antagonist became a part of the alien system and joined in taking over the town.

Barbie

This movie places second on my list, this movie talks about the struggle women go through and really opened my eyes to their struggles.

I do plan on showing my mother this movie, I feel that she would really be able to resonate with the message it gives.

The Ballad of Songbirds and Snakes

This movie cames in third, there were some part of the movie that did a lot of talking and felt like they were taking a while to get tot he point.

The way Viola Davis delivers the lines as the evil scientist was perfection. She really embodied the cahracter and delivered her lines with passion.
